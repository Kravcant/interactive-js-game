# Bladebound Realms

Draw your sword forward and slay the beasts of the Infernal Wastes! Click on the enemies to attack them; Defeat the enemy to move onto 
the next level. A boss awaits you at every 5 levels. Use the gold that you've obtained from fights to increase your damage. Complete
level 25 to finish your quest, or continue smiting enemies in endless mode.
